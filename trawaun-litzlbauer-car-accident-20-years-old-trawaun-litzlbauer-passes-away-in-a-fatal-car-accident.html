Trawaun Litzlbauer Car Accident; 20 Years Old Trawaun Litzlbauer Passes Away In A Fatal Car Accident

An automobile collision that took place in Jackson County on Wednesday afternoon resulted in the deaths of two individuals.

Two vehicles were involved in a collision that occurred in the 2200 block of U.S. 50, according to the Jackson County Sheriff’s Department dispatch, which received a call about the incident at midday.

The two vehicles that were involved in the collision were a white Hyundai Sonata from 2007 that was traveling west on United States Route 50 and a white Chevrolet Pickup from 2005 that was traveling east on United States Route 50.

Both the driver of the Hyundai, Trawaun Litzlbauer, who was 20 years old, and the passenger, Stanley Baughman Jr., who was 67 years old, ended up passing away as a result of injuries they got in the collision. Both were pronounced dead at the scene, according to the cops that responded.

The driver of the Chevrolet, Charles Callahan, who is 43 years old, was taken to the University of Louisville Hospital and is currently undergoing medical treatment.

In addition to the Indiana State Police and Jackson County, the Brownstown Fire Department, the Brownstown Police Department, and Indiana Conservation all provided assistance during the collision.
