Which are the best Big Sean quotes?

Big Sean is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter, who is signed with Kanye West’s GOOD Music in 2007, Def Jam Recordings in 2008, and Roc Nation in 2014. Throughout his career, he has won several awards including the ‘MTV Music Awards’, and ‘BET Awards’. 

Sean is first rose to international fame in 2011, after the release of his debut studio album, ‘Finally Famous’. He has collaborated with various high-profile artists over the years, such as ‘A$AP Ferg’, ‘Chris Brown‘, ‘Chance The Rapper‘, and ‘Jeremih‘.
