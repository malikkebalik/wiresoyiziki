Parent: How to Reset Time4Learning Student Login Password?

When you lose the password for your official time4learning parent account, you shouldn’t worry. Instead, you should reset your password to start the process of getting your password back. If you don’t know how, please read the guide below.

  • Please click on the “Parents” tab on your device’s login page. Parents can log in to their accounts and change their passwords.
  • Now, click on the button that says “Forgot Password?”

  • Please put your actual email address here.
  • Then click Password Reset.
  • Your account will be checked, and you’ll get an email to reset your password.

  • It’ll take you to a new page. Make a new password now.
  • Then you must enter the password again to confirm it.
  • Hit “Submit” to finish the process.
