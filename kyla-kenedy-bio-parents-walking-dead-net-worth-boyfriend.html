Kyla Kenedy: Acting Career

There is no indication of Kenedy’s life before her acting career was launched. So, we cannot say for sure if she wanted to be an actress or pursue another career. Nonetheless, she made her Hollywood debut at the age of 8 from a TV series titled CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.

The film was directed by Logan Thomas and starred Aric Cushing.

That same year, the American actress appeared in the TV movie, Raising Izzie. Her lead role in the show helped her win Young Artist Awards in 2013. However, it was her appearance in the horror series The Walking Dead that earned her great success.

Likewise, Kenedy played the role of young Mika Samuels in the series from 2013-2015, starring Andrew Lincoln, Norman Reedus, Lauren Cohan, and many others. After the series, she appeared in the 2016 ABC’s series, Speechless as Dylan DiMeo. The series also featured actors like Micah Fowler, Minnie Driver, and John Ross Bowie.
