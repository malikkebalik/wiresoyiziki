Mackenzie Edwards Throws Shade At Maci Bookout and Ryan Edwards Following Their Onscreen Reunion

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Credit: MTV

In a surprising turn of events, Ryan Edwards returned to Teen Mom: Next Chapter, despite being previously fired by MTV. Following his onscreen reunion with Maci Bookout, it looks like Mackenzie Standifer‘s feathers were ruffled… and she’s apparently throwing shade.

Teen Mom: The Next Chapter shows Ryan Edwards is back in town.

In a move that shocked many fans, Maci Bookout‘s ex returned to MTV to film an emotional update, and things have gotten intense.

“I never imagined we would be in a place where this would be happening,” Maci spilled in a recent Teen Mom: The Next Chapter confessional.

Earlier this year, Ryan was arrested for harassment and violating an order of protection that was filed by Mackenzie. He was also charged with drug possession after “two bags of suspected narcotics” were found on him when he was taken into custody.

Numerous 911 calls were made from Mackenzie and her father due to claims of domestic violence. She asked authorities to meet her away from her house because she did not feel safe.

Shortly after that, Ryan was sentenced to time in rehab. Producers decided that this storyline was too good to pass up and he began filming again for Teen Mom: The Next Chapter.

Credit: MTV

“MTV was all over this,” a production insider informed The Ashley’s Reality Roundup back in March. “With all of this drama happening with [the Edwards family], they wasted no time bringing him back and were surprised he agreed, actually.”

According to the outlet, it was confirmed that the former couple filmed together at a Tennessee restaurant earlier in the spring. That appears to be the encounter featured in the new preview clip.

In the video, Ryan is resting his head on the table when Maci walks up. The father of three reveals that he will be heading to a rehab facility in Austin, Texas.
