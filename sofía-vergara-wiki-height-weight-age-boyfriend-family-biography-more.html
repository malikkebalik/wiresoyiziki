A Colombian-American actress Sofia Vergara started her acting career as a co-host of a couple of Spanish-language television shows on Univisión. To achieve success and make her dreams come true, she went through a lot of hardships and proved that hard work and perseverance paid off in the end.

She was born Sofia Margarita Vergara on July 10, 1972. Her role of Gloria Delgado-Pritchett on the ABC series ‘Modern Family’, led her to significant roles in movies like ‘Chasing Papi’, ‘Four Brothers’, ‘Meet the Browns’, ‘Madea Goes to Jail’, ‘Hot Pursuit’, ‘Happy Feet Two’, ‘The Smurfs’, ‘The Three Stooges’, and ‘Machete Kills’. Forbes ranked her 32nd on the list of most powerful women in the world.

Once a photographer spotted Sofia walking on a Colombian beach and offered her work and she started her career as a model. From 1995 to 1998, she did her first major job on television was co-hosting the Univisión travel show, ‘Fuera de Serie’.

In 2008, she appeared in the movie ‘Madea Goes to Jail’ for which she received a nomination for an ALMA Award for ‘Best Actress in Film’ for her performance. In 2009, she got her breakthrough role as the sexy and assertive Gloria Delgado-Pritchett on the ABC sitcom ‘Modern Family’ which became an instant hit and earned her multiple awards and nominations.

Apart from modeling for different brands, she has launched her own clothing line and jewelry collection. She has worked as a voice artist in the 2013 animated sci-fi, ‘Escape from Planet Earth’.

In 1990, Sofia Vergara married her high-school sweetheart, Joe Gonzalez but in 1993, she separated from her husband. In 2015, she got married to Joe Manganiello in Palm Beach, Florida. She has a son Manolo Gonzalez-Ripoll Vergara from her first husband.
