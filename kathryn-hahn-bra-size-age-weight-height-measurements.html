Biography:

Kathryn Hahn was born on July 23, 1973 in Westchester, Illinois, U.S. She studied at the Northwestern University where she earned a bachelor’s degree in theatre and later attended Yale.

From 2001 to 2007, she became known for her role as Lily Lebowski in the television series Crossing Jordan. She has since appeared in other television programs including Parks and Recreation, Girls, and The Newsroom, among others.

She also gained notice for her roles in the big screen. Her film work includes How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days, Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy, Revolutionary Road, We’re the Millers and many others.
