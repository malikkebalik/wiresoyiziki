Alleged Details Revealed of What David Eason Did to Jace Evans

Credit: MTV

Court documents revealed the shocking details of what David Eason did to Jenelle Evans‘ son Jace that led to a CPS investigation and a warrant for his arrest.

David Eason is a wanted man after a CPS investigation against him and Jenelle Evans.

There is currently a warrant out for David for physically assaulting Jace Evans. Disturbing new details about the attack have now been revealed.

On September 29th, the 14-year-old was reported missing for a third time. Soon after, police began treating the case as an investigation into possible neglect.

According to TMZ, Jace escaped through his window and went out into the woods that surround the family’s home.

Jenelle told law enforcement that her son left the house without a phone. The police showed up to their home around 8:00 p.m., TMZ noted.

The 31-year-old soon shared with her followers on social media that her oldest son had been found safe. He was then removed from the Easons’ custody and placed back with Jenelle’s mother, Barbara.

The CPS investigation found that David committed physical harm against Jace. Their evidence is outlined in court documents.

The Sun obtained records that outline David’s physical assault.

According to the paperwork, it happened on September 28th.

“The defendant unlawfully and willingly did, being the parent of Jace V Evans, who was a child, less than 16 years of age, inflict physical injury on that child,” the court documents explain.
