Future Brings $1 Million In Cash To A Studio Session With DJ Khaled

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  246 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Everyone finds inspiration in different ways. Some like to talk to themselves in an effort to pump themselves up before a big moment. Others like to compare themselves to other people in order to find inspiration to strive for more. But one artist likes to motivate himself in a very, very expensive way.

Future, who is fresh off of back to back No. 1 albums that he dropped in February, was in the studio with DJ Khaled earlier this week, in order to help Khaled on his forthcoming Grateful album. While we can expect Future's features on Khaled's album to be absolute bangers, what was noteworthy about the Atlanta native's session with Khaled was what he brought along with him.

TREVOR COLLENS/AFP/Getty Images

Khaled, who is very active on social media, made a series of Instagram posts showing Future with extremely large amounts of cash. The posts show stacks of money on tables and in bags. Khaled went on to state in the caption of one of the posts, that Future rapped he had a million dollars of cash on him in the studio.

Well, if you looked at the photos, it's clear that Future wasn't lying. Hey, if that's what the man needs to get inspired…

As for DJ Khaled's album, it will be executive produced by his infant son, Asahd, who was born in October of last year. Grateful doesn't have a release date yet, but is expected to drop later this year.
